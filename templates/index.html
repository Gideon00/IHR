Welcome to IHR

Welcome to Intelligent Health Record (IHR), the future of healthcare documentation. Our AI-powered platform is designed to enhance the efficiency of healthcare providers by automating tedious administrative tasks, allowing more time for patient care.

Why Choose IHR?

Reduce Physician Burnout

IHR transcribes doctor-patient conversations into detailed clinical notes, saving valuable time and reducing the stress of documentation.

Enhance Diagnostic Accuracy

Our AI-driven differential diagnosis support provides physicians with accurate diagnostic suggestions based on patient history and symptoms.

Ensure Patient Safety

Real-time drug-drug interaction alerts help prevent potential medication errors, ensuring the safety and well-being of patients.

Integrate Seamlessly

IHR extracts relevant data from scanned paper records, integrating it into digital health records for a comprehensive view of patient history.

Key Features

Automated Documentation

Effortlessly transcribe and generate detailed clinical notes from doctor-patient conversations.

Differential Diagnosis Support

Receive AI-driven diagnostic suggestions to enhance clinical decision-making.

Drug-Drug Interaction Alerts

Get real-time alerts for potential drug-drug interactions to ensure patient safety.

Patient History Extraction

Extract and integrate data from both digital and scanned paper records seamlessly.

How It Works

  1. Voice Recognition: Securely record doctor-patient conversations.
  2. AI Processing: Our advanced AI models transcribe conversations into detailed clinical notes.
  3. Data Integration: Extract and integrate patient history from digital and scanned records.
  4. Real-Time Alerts: Receive immediate alerts for potential drug-drug interactions.
